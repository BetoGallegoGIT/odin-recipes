Burger

Description

BBQ burger with cheddar & bacon

Ingredients

  • Burger patty
  • Burger buns
  • Onion
  • Bacon
  • BBQ sauce
  • Cheddar

Steps

  1. Cook the pattys
  2. Cook the bacon
  3. Cook the onion (cut in little squares first)
  4. Add everything between the buns
  5. Add the cheddar and the sauce

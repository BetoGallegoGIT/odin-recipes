Pancakes

Description

Healthy protein pancakes made with banana

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 60gr oatmeal
  • Milk
  • 1 banana

Steps

  1. Get a bowl and crack 2 eggs on it
  2. Add a splash of milk
  3. Smash the banana
  4. Grind the oatmeal
  5. Add both into the bowl
  6. MIX MIX MIX
  7. Cook them one by one in a frying pan
